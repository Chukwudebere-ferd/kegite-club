About Us

Brief History

The Kegites Club International, founded in 1962, is built on the principles of unity, diversity, and camaraderie, with the motto "Unity in Diversity" a pointer to our previous motto which is "the Basis of African Unity is in the Palm wine".

Owing to many developments in the structure of Anamenuebonyi Hemisphere our beloved Mother Hemisphere, on 12th of March 2026 with the imprimatur of our Mother Hemisphere, Anaenuebonyi Hemisphere, this our noble Hemisphere by name Kegites Club Supreme Anambra Hemisphere was established with the modus operandi of fostering unity and continuity of peaceful recognition and practice of African tradition mainly in our educational institutions at the Anambra State level.

Our Vision

We envisage an era of unity in a world of diversity.

Our Mission

The mission of the Kegites Club Supreme Anambra Hemisphere is to uphold, preserve, and promote African cultural heritage, fostering unity, peace, and "Unity in Diversity" among members and the human race. As a socio-cultural club, it aims to relieve stress, encourage social interaction, and promote honest living while standing against racism, tribalism, and social vices.

Our Values

Our values include (but are not limited to):

  1. Cultural Heritage Preservation: Promoting African traditions, music, language, and the appreciation of palm wine as a traditional drink that survived the adulteration of colonialism.
  2. Unity in Diversity: The core motto, emphasizing harmony across different cultures, backgrounds, and beliefs.
  3. Comradeship and Mutual Support: Encouraging brother and sister hood and supporting members in times of difficulty.
  4. Humility and Simple Living: Encouraging honest living and providing a, "comradely," social space to relieve the stress of academic or professional life through gyrations and mutations.
  5. Inclusivity and Peace: The club is known for its open atmosphere and discourages violence, tribalism, racism, and secret cultism.
  6. Exploration of our cultural heritage and traditions.

So far, our Hemisphere cum the Club has grown into an international community, upholding African cultural heritage and promoting social bonding. We're proud of our achievements and look forward to continued growth and excellence.
